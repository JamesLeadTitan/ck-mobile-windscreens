Classic Car Specialists

Classic & Vintage Car Windscreen Specialists

CK Mobile Windscreens understands the unique needs of classic car enthusiasts. With 50 years of experience, we specialise in replacing windscreens and other vehicle glass for classic models — from pre-war cars to the iconic vehicles of the 1970s and 80s.

Whether it's a cherished family heirloom or a prized collector's item, we ensure every piece of automotive glass is fitted with precision and care. Our stock includes glass for models from the 1920s onwards, helping to maintain the authenticity and value of your classic car.

  • Front windscreens, back windows, door windows
  • Quarter lights and vents
  • Rubber surrounds and trims
  • Original or aftermarket glass options
  • Custom sourcing for genuinely rare parts
  • Supply & fit, or supply only if you prefer your own fitter

Why choose a specialist?

Classic car glass is not the same as modern vehicle glass. Dimensions, curvature, tinting and rubber profiles are often unique to specific models and years. Getting it wrong can destroy authenticity — and value. We've been doing this since 1975, so we know what matters.

Can't find the glass?

If we don't have the glass in stock, we will make every effort to source it — or have it custom-made. Don't assume it can't be found. Call us and we'll tell you honestly what's possible.

How We Work

Glass Replacement That Respects Your Vehicle

We treat every classic car with the same care its owner does. That means using the correct glass specification for the year and model — not a modern approximation — and fitting it with the correct rubbers and trims.

Our experienced technicians understand how classic car glass differs from modern equivalents. We work methodically, protecting surrounding bodywork, and we take as long as the job needs rather than rushing to the next booking.

  • Highly experienced technicians — not just windscreen fitters
  • Correct glass specification verified before ordering
  • Period-correct rubber seals and trims used
  • Bodywork protection throughout the process
  • Mobile service — we come to your garage or home
  • Available 7 days a week across a 100-mile radius

Supply only option

If you have your own trusted fitter or prefer to fit the glass yourself, we're happy to supply the glass, rubber, and trim without installation. We'll make sure you get exactly the right specification.

Covering a 100-mile radius

Based in Malvern, we cover Cheltenham, Worcester, Hereford, Birmingham and beyond. If you're unsure whether we cover your area, just call — we'll tell you straight.
