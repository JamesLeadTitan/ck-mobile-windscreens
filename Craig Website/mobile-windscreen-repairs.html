Chipped or Cracked?

Windscreen Chip & Crack Repair — We Come to You

CK Mobile Windscreens has been repairing and replacing windscreens since 1975, bringing 50 years of expertise to Malvern and the surrounding areas. Whether it's a chip, crack, or full replacement, we understand the importance of keeping your vehicle safe and roadworthy.

Our mobile service means we come to you — saving you time and hassle. From modern vehicles to cherished classic cars, we handle every job with precision and care, ensuring your glass is restored to its best condition.

  • Chips smaller than 1 inch (2.54cm) — ideal for repair
  • Cracks assessed and repaired where possible
  • Damage near the driver's line of sight assessed individually
  • Full windscreen replacement arranged if repair isn't viable
  • No need to leave home or work

Will my insurer pay?

Many comprehensive insurance policies cover windscreen repair at no cost to you — often with no excess. We can advise you on the repair process; contact your insurer to confirm your policy details.

When to repair vs replace?

Chips under 1 inch (2.54cm) can usually be repaired invisibly. Larger damage, cracks spreading across the windscreen, or damage in the driver's direct line of vision will likely require a full replacement.

Our Method

UV Resin Windscreen Repair Technology

Using UV-hardened resin and state-of-the-art ESPRIT equipment, we deliver repairs that are both durable and seamless. Our aim is to restore the structural strength of your windscreen, preventing further damage and maintaining perfect visibility.

The repair process is quick — typically completed in under 30 minutes — with no need to wait for glass to cure. You'll be back on the road the same day.

  • High-end ESPRIT repair equipment used on every job
  • UV-hardened resin for lasting, invisible results
  • Restoration of windscreen structural integrity
  • Prevents chips from spreading into full cracks
  • Completed in under 30 minutes in most cases
  • No waiting — drive away the same day

Why repair quickly?

A small chip left untreated can spread into a crack within days — especially with temperature changes, road vibration, or a wet weather event. What costs £30–£60 to repair today could require a full replacement costing hundreds.

Driving with a damaged windscreen can also result in a roadworthiness fail and potentially invalidate your insurance. Call us as soon as you notice damage.
